About the event

The theme of our event is “Start a New Chapter” because we aim to create a new TASSEL school in a rural Cambodian village in the Battambang province called Samlot. The proceeds from our event will go towards laying the foundation, building classrooms, hiring local teachers, providing transportation, and giving hundreds of kids the opportunity to escape the poverty cycle. We hope you purchase a ticket and donate to this wonderful cause.

About TASSEL

TASSEL (“Teaching And Sharing Skills to Enrich Lives”) is a U.S. 501(c)(3) public charity established in 2012. Initially TASSEL's sole activity was having U.S.-based, trained volunteers provide free English classes over video conferencing to the poor in rural Cambodia. But it has quickly evolved to include actively encouraging, recruiting, training and funding high potential Cambodian adults to become teachers as well as providing food aid, medical care access and other support services to the poorest families who have limited capabilities to earn income. More information about our organization can be found at tasselcambodia.org.
